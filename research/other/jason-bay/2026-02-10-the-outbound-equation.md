## Framework outline (as presented)

- Value-first offers as the entry point of every touch.
- Phone execution as a separately-mastered channel (not "just a step in a cadence").
- Email as its own excellence bar (copy, targeting, deliverability).
- Social as a supporting channel — not a lone lever.
- Sales leadership carries direct responsibility for pipeline creation, not just enablement.
- Predictable/repeatable outbound systems over individual heroics.
- Fundamentals-over-tools stance for consistent results.
