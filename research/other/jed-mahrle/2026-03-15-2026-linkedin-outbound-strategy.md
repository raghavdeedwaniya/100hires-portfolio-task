## Key points

- ~30% reply rate on LinkedIn across 25 clients (healthcare, SaaS, landscaping, IT services); ranges 15–45%.
- Volume cap: 30 messages/day/person to avoid LinkedIn throttling — forces a quality-over-quantity discipline.
- Lead selection prioritizes intent signals: website visitors, job changers, event attendees, top-performing customer segments, and prospects showing LinkedIn network activity.
- Messaging framework: (1) blank connection request, (2) open-ended pain question, (3) helpful resource (guide/template/checklist), (4) soft CTA follow-up.
- Style: casual/lowercase text, short, no email-length paragraphs.
- Conversion rules: reply inside 10 minutes; ask for the meeting the moment interest appears; 4–5 follow-ups across channels.
- Tools named: Kondo, HeyReach.
