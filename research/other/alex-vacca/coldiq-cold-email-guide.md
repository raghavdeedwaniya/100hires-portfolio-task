## Key tactics

- Trigger events to source from: hiring spikes, product launches, funding, leadership changes, office expansions.
- Build hypotheses per segment (e.g., remote companies → retention pain), then test with campaigns.
- Filter accounts (Apollo etc.) by title/industry/geo/size before enrichment.
- Personalize at scale using job descriptions + news to generate per-account email snippets.
- Cap sending to 20–30 emails/day per domain to protect reputation.
- Waterfall email validation before sending.
- Copy: short, no links/images, personalized opener, 2–3 follow-ups minimum.
- Infra: SPF/DKIM/DMARC configured; use separate domains; warm-up software.
