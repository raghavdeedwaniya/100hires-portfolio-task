Date range for "active": content within the last ~6 months from 2026-07-06.

## Selection bar
- **Practitioner** — runs (or ran) the motion; company/agency/tool/team behind them.
- **Active** — on-topic content in roughly the last 6 months.
- **Collectable** — 5–6 of 10 have YouTube/video-podcast footprint so transcripts can be pulled via API.
- **Complementary angles** — messaging, deliverability, list building, sequencing/tech, cold calling, ops/metrics.
