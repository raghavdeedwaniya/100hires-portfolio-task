## Key takeaways
- A Belkins/Gong study of 85 million cold emails found that including a strategic offer in the CTA drove a 28% lift in positive replies that converted into meetings.
- Buyers are "jaded" and will not take a meeting just to see a demo or hear about your services, so the demo-request CTA is no longer compelling enough on its own.
- Every cold email CTA should answer the prospect's question: "What will I get out of this meeting if I choose not to buy your product or service?"
