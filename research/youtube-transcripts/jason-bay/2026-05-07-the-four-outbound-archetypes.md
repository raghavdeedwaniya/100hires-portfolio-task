## Key takeaways
- Jason names four outbound rep archetypes: the Grinder, the Procrastinator, the Automator (aka "cyborgs"), and the Owner.
- Grinders overshoot activity targets (e.g., 120 dials when 100 is mandated) but, counterintuitively, are not typically the most consistent pipe-gen performers.
- Procrastinators, where most sellers actually fall, only prospect when they need pipeline "yesterday," creating an emotional roller coaster and inconsistency despite some being top performers.
- Automators ("cyborgs") refuse to send an email unless ChatGPT or Claude writes it for them, over-relying on AI.
- Owners are the ideal: they treat their book of business like they are the CEO of it and pipe-gen constantly because "a business cannot live without pipeline — it needs oxygen."
