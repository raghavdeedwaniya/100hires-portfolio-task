## Key takeaways
- Prospecting boils down to three skills: picking the right accounts and people to reach out to, writing a good email, and making a good cold call.
- The vast majority of prospecting problems are attributable to reaching out to the wrong accounts and people — not the cold call language or cold email language.
- Diagnosis rule: when outbound isn't working, fix targeting first before rewriting scripts or email copy.
