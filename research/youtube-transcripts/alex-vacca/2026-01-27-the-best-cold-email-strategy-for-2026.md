## Key takeaways
- Build lists on intent signals (recent funding, marketing-role hiring, Shopify Plus/Klaviyo usage, founder LinkedIn posts about conversion problems) — Alex targets ~200 in-market accounts instead of 7M, expecting 3-5% reply rate vs sub-1%.
- Recommended sourcing stack: Clay to pull data, LinkedIn Sales Navigator for hiring, Crunchbase for funding, and 6sense for solution-research signals — start with two, not all four.
- Lead with "give first" assets like a 12-minute recorded website audit, a lead-gen breakdown doc, or six campaign ideas, instead of "want to hop on a call," to trigger reciprocity and a click.
- Use RB2B or Vector to deanonymize the resulting website visitors, then auto-push them into LinkedIn/Google retargeting for 30 days plus a HubSpot/Attio nurture — one click becomes 7-13 touchpoints.
- Wire CRM alerts so a combo (visits pricing page + watches demo + opens 3 emails) pings sales immediately, and use Trigify to log LinkedIn profile views and post engagement back to the CRM.
- Alex's agency scaled from 30K to 557K/month by running this four-lever loop, turning 1,000 emails into 30-50 tracked engagements and ~3 deals instead of the old 20 replies / 1 deal.
