## Key takeaways
- Only take cold email clients whose customer LTV is $10K+ and who sell B2B via email; Jay charges $2,500-$5,000/month retainers and locks in a 3-month minimum because cold email needs ~30 days of warm-up before real sending.
- For the probe.dev client, his team scraped GitHub open-source contributors from repositories relevant to video-file APIs and wrote developer-to-developer copy, generating 70 opportunities in month one at 3% reply rates.
- All clients live on Email Bison (flat $600/month with unlimited sub-accounts and unlimited leads on Jay's own subdomain/server) rather than Instantly or SmartLead, which share warm-up pools with spammers and balloon in cost as leads scale.
- Home server "Zeus" (a ~$10,000 build, or ~$50/month on Hostinger for beginners) runs open-source scrapers with proxies, hosts email verification for free (replacing Million Verifier), and powers Consulti's B2B/local/creator lead databases.
- A per-client "nano Claude" instance on Zeus checks Email Bison for replies every few minutes, uses each client's knowledge base to draft answers, and lets clients chat with it to adjust FAQs - replacing the VA reply team at zero incremental software cost.
- New agency economics: fixed costs of $599/mo Bison + $100/mo Claude Code per team member enable scaling to 10 clients at $30K MRR without adding software or headcount, since one client covers all infrastructure.
