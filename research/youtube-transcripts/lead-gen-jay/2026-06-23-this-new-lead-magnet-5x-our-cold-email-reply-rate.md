## Key takeaways
- The "SaaS magnet" reverse lead magnet CTA - e.g., "I took a look at your LinkedIn and have some great book angles that could land you as a bestseller, want to see them?" - drove an 8% reply rate for Gundy's book campaign, generating a sale and three signups in week one before she paused it for being too hot.
- Always send the SaaS magnet link with the prospect's LinkedIn URL pre-filled as a URL parameter so the tool auto-scans on click; forcing them to paste their own LinkedIn "hammers your conversions."
- Every click drops the prospect into Google, Facebook, TikTok, and LinkedIn retargeting pixels, so even non-converters get followed by dirt-cheap retargeting ads for months.
- Consulti's free Lead Magnet Builder generates a PRD (product requirements document) from your offer that you download as an .md file and hand to Bolt.new or Lovable, which builds and hosts the working app in about 5 minutes on generous free tiers.
- Apify's LinkedIn scraper actor costs ~$10 per 1,000 profiles, and pairing it with OpenRouter (one card, all LLMs at Anthropic/OpenAI cost) is the cheapest fulfillment backend for the SaaS magnet.
- Since Email Bison lacks a native reply agent, use an n8n flow ("replyji") that classifies replies, tags interested leads, and drafts an HTML reply containing the LinkedIn-prefilled SaaS magnet URL automatically.
