## Key takeaways
- Jay runs his entire cold email stack (Instantly/Email Bison, Inbox Insiders for mailboxes/domains, Consulti or Apify for leads, Turso/Supabase/Neon for databasing) from Claude Code skills using just API keys stored in `.env.local`, eliminating logins to Apollo, Clay, N8N, and Make.
- For agencies he recommends Email Bison ($600/month with unlimited sub-accounts) over Instantly, and for lead scraping only two Apify actors made the cut: the Code Crafter leads finder and a specific Google Maps scraper.
- His "list optimize" skill replaces Clay by chaining qualification, normalization, verification, then Perplexity Sonar research plus Claude personalization only on qualified+verified leads, and it pre-tests personalization fields against the sequencer to add fallbacks before sending.
- The "auto learn / auto improve" skill (based on Karpathy's auto-research repo with 79k stars) makes copywriting skills self-critique and rewrite until output meets the ICP/copy parameters, running loops overnight on the $200/month Max plan.
- Reverse lead magnets built via Consulti's lead-magnet builder plus Bolt or Lovable took his clients from ~2% to ~5% reply rates by passing the prospect's LinkedIn URL as a query parameter so the micro-SaaS auto-generates a personalized result on click.
- To bypass Claude's 5-hour usage limit mid-workflow, he logs out of one Max account and `/login` to a second Max account, then types "continue" and the session resumes where it left off.
