## Key takeaways
- Stop opening with "I saw you're hiring for [role]" — Eric says as soon as prospects see that line they write you off; it's 2022-era table stakes.
- Instead, scrape the full job descriptions and use AI to mine "the gold" inside them — e.g., 5 open enterprise AE reqs signals an upmarket push; CISO + security engineer reqs mentioning compliance signal a SOC 2 / enterprise-compliance need.
- Source the jobs data from Clay, Apify's Indeed scraper, or parallel.ai; alternatives include predictleads for a LinkedIn Jobs API.
- To build the extraction prompt, open a Loom, read through 10 real relevant job posts out loud narrating what's interesting, then feed the transcript to an LLM to author the prompt.
- Run the extraction on OpenRouter with open-source models for cost, or DeepInfra / vast.ai (rented GPUs) if you need even cheaper at high volume.
- Write the outbound message the same way: hand-write 3-10 example messages from real job-description insights, then have AI generate the messaging prompt from those examples — and never mention the job req itself in the email.
