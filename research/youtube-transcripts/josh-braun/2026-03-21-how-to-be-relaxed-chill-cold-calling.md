## Key takeaways
- Model your mindset on MLB hitters — even the best only get on base ~36% of the time, and they treat strikeouts and hits as part of the same experience without letting either phase them.
- Detach from any single call outcome; focusing on "getting on base" makes two or three bad calls feel devastating.
- Trust the volume: Josh found that 46 dials reliably produced at least two or three positive 2–3 minute conversations, and sometimes a booked meeting.
- The muscle to train is stepping up to the plate and "swinging 46 or 47 times" — focus on what you control (the dials) and tune out what you don't (the reactions).
